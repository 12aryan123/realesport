IN-GAME QUALIFIERS

Players who have registered for iQOO BATTLEGROUNDS MOBILE INDIA SERIES 2021 will have to play 15 matches with their registered team members over the given time period. The top 10 of 15 matches will be considered for evaluation and scored accordingly. In case of a tie, certain parameters such as finishes, survival time, accuracy, etc will be considered. Top 1024 teams will qualify for the next round.
Note: Only the first 15 matches played with registered team members shall be deemed valid.

ONLINE QUALIFIERS

Round 1
The top 1024 squads qualifying from Online Qualifiers will be seeded into 64 groups. Each group will have to play 2 matches each on the specified dates. The top 8 teams from each group will further qualify for the Round 2 of online qualifiers.

Round 2
The top 512 teams from Online Qualifiers Round 1 will be seeded into 32 groups. Each group will have to play 2 matches each on the specified dates. The top 7 teams from each group will proceed to online qualifiers Round 3.

Round 3
Top 224 teams from Online Qualifiers Round 3 along with 32 invited teams will be seeded into 16 groups. Each group will have to play 3 matches each on the specified dates following which the top 4 teams will qualify for Quarter Finals.

QUARTER FINALS

The top 64 teams qualifying from Online Qualifiers Round 3 will be divided into 4 groups. Each group will have to play 6 matches each on the specified dates. The top 6 teams from each group will further qualify to the Semi-Finals.

SEMI FINALS

24 teams qualified from the Quarter Finals will be divided into 3 groups who will compete against each other in a round robin format. A set of 6 Round Robins will be played by each group. The top 16 squads with the highest cumulative points will move towards the Grand Finals.

GRAND FINALS

Top 16 teams qualifying from Semi Finals will play 24 matches over a time period of 4 days. The squad with the highest cumulative score at the end of 24 matches shall be declared as the winner of iQOO BATTLEGROUNDS MOBILE INDIA SERIES 2021.
